Kapraw

Pork-rice version of Kapraw with fried egg on top.

People always say Kapraw is the national cuisine in Thailand. It's flavor certainly tells a lot about the country. It's can be extremely spicy which is reflective of the hot tropical climate. It's usually salty but some variation can be led by sweet taste. It can vary depending on the taste of the local vicinity.

Ingredients

  • Meat of choice: ground pork, chicken, beef or seafood
  • Holy basil
  • Garlic
  • Chilli
  • Fish sauce
  • Sugar
  • Oyster sauce
  • Cooking oil

Steps

  1. Pour 2-3 spoons of cooking oil into a frying pan.
  2. Heat the pan to high temperature.
  3. Put 2-3 clove of peeled garlic in. Cook for 1 minute.
  4. Put the meat into the pan, stir fry until moderately cooked
  5. Put holy basil and chili in.
  6. Follow by a spoon of oyster sauce, half to a spoon of fish sauce and a pinch of sugar.
  7. Continue to stir fry until the meat is well-cook.
